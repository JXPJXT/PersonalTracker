## 🔥 Next Steps to Complete the Vision

To continue building out the ultimate integrated personal operating system:

### Phase 1: The "Command Center" Dashboard (Global Analytics)
Currently, data is split by pages. The Command Center will be the new default homepage, providing a 10,000-foot view of your entire life.
- **Correlated Insights:** "On days you sleep 8+ hours, your productivity rating is 20% higher."
- **Week at a glance:** Overlay Habit completions on top of Study Hours to see if one cannibalizes the other.
- **Widgets:** Customizable grid (drag-and-drop) containing your Exam Countdown, today's Habits, Recent Resources, and active Tasks.

### Phase 2: Advanced Notes & Database Linking
Now that the core Notion block-editor is fully implemented in Notes and Journal:
- **Bi-directional linking:** Typing `[[Concept]]` in your Daily Journal automatically links it to your Knowledge Map.
- **Embeds:** Embed tasks or calendar events directly into your Notes.
- **Multi-view Databases:** Kanban, Gallery, and Calendar views for Resources and Notes.

### Phase 3: Advanced Habit & Goal Systems
- **Conditional Goals:** "If I study 4 hours, autofill my 'Deep Work' habit."
- **Progressive Overload:** Automatically increase target units (e.g., Target goes from 10 pages reading to 15 next week).
- **Streak Freezes & Gamification:** Allow Grace Days to avoid breaking long streaks, add badges for multi-domain streaks.

### Phase 4: Extended Life Integrations
- **Finance/Expense Tracker:** A simple ledger linked to your Daily Journal.
- **Fitness Module:** Evolve the generic Habit tracker for dedicated workout routines/PRs.
- **Read-it-Later Inbox:** A quick-capture extension or hotkey to instantly dump links into the Resource Map to be triaged later.
